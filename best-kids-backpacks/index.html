Quick answer

The Pottery Barn Kids MacKenzie Backpack is our top pick for its premium materials and customizable options that let kids express themselves.

For a reliable everyday classic, the JanSport SuperBreak Plus offers unbeatable value, while the L.L.Bean Original Book Pack is built to survive years of rough handling.

A school backpack is a kid’s daily companion, carrying everything from homework to lunch, gym clothes to art projects. The right one balances comfort, durability, and enough organization to keep chaos at bay. Weight matters: a bag that’s too heavy empty will only get worse as the year goes on. Look for padded shoulder straps, a breathable back panel, and a sturdy bottom that won’t sag. Beyond durability, consider your child’s age and needs. Little ones need easy-to-use zippers and fun designs; older students want laptop compartments and multiple pockets. And never underestimate the power of letting your child pick the color or pattern, a backpack they love is more likely to stay off the floor and actually get used.
